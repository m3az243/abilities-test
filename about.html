Who are we

Simple training platform for capabilities test

This platform is designed to provide a fast and clear training experience that simulates the capabilities test style, with an easy Arabic interface, accurate timer, and automatic data saving inside the browser.

work of student Abdulaziz Abdullah Al-Qadi and his colleagues: Basem Al-Humaidi, Mohammed Saud Al-Subaiti, Anas Mahrous Al-Khadi, Mazen Al-Rimi, Motez Mohammed, Abdullah Kamal Mahna (Ketchup), Anas Abkar, Mazen Al-Malki, Ahmed Shrahan. Al-Hadabiya Secondary School, Class 3/3.

Our goal is to provide the user with a comfortable experience that helps them practice, measure their level, and review their mistakes without complexity or many steps.
